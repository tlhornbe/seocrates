SEOCrates Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 13, 2026

1. Data Access

SEOCrates accesses the Document Object Model (DOM) of the active browser tab to extract technical SEO metadata, heading structures, and page content.

2. Local Processing

All data extraction and semantic analysis (vector embedding generation) are performed locally on your device using WebAssembly (WASM).

3. No Data Collection or Transmission

SEOCrates does not collect, store, or transmit any user data, website content, or browsing history to external servers. Your data never leaves your machine.

4. Third Parties

No data is shared with third parties, as no data is ever collected by the developer.
