SEOCrates Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 13, 2026

1. Data Access Disclosure

SEOCrates accesses the Document Object Model (DOM) of the active browser tab to extract technical SEO metadata, heading structures, and link data. This access is performed solely for the purpose of providing on-page SEO auditing and alignment scoring.

2. Local Processing & Privacy

All semantic analysis and vector embedding generation are performed locally on your device using WebAssembly (WASM) and Transformers.js. No data is ever transmitted to a server or external third party. Your content stays entirely within your browser environment.

3. No History or Content Storage

SEOCrates does not collect, store, or transmit your browsing history, website content, or personal information. The extension provides real-time analysis without persisting your data beyond the active session.

4. Third Parties

Because no data is collected, no data is shared with third parties. SEOCrates is built for privacy and performance, operating as a standalone tool on your machine.
